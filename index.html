PROGRAMMES AVAILABLE

Beginners

We run beginners' friendly lectures for those who are just starting out in learning the Arabic Language, and it ranges from identification of the Arabic alphabets, to various arabic forms and many more.

Intermediate

This is for those who have completed the beginners (basic) part of the Arabic learning process, this includes the introduction to grammar, morphology and many other subjects to enhance fluency in the Arabic language.

Advanced

This entails going deeper into the knowledge of the Arabic language, with the introduction of more intriguing and challenging subjects that guides one through the journey of Arabic Studies.
